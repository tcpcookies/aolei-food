About Us

Our story, our values, and our commitment to delivering premium frozen bakery products worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Shenzhen Aolei Food Co., Ltd. began with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of Portuguese egg tarts to bakeries and food service businesses around the world, in a format that is convenient, consistent, and easy to use.

What started as a small workshop has grown into a modern frozen food manufacturing facility located in the Shanziding Industrial Zone, Huiyang District, Huizhou City, Guangdong Province — one of China's most dynamic food production hubs.

Today, we export our premium tart crusts to over 15 countries across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, North America, and Europe. Our commitment to handmade quality combined with modern food safety standards has earned us the trust of bakeries, supermarket chains, and food distributors worldwide.
